# Hands Heart Website Redesign PRD

## 1. Overview

This project is the design and development of a new website for Hands Heart. The goal is to create a simple, modern online presence that puts the heart hands drawing at the center of the experience. The website will use a clean white layout, restrained typography, and small moments of color to let the symbol stand on its own while showcasing the brand and its products.

The new website is being built to replace the existing site with something that feels more intentional, trustworthy, and easier to use. It will give visitors a clear understanding of what Hands Heart is, make it easy to browse and purchase products through Shopify, and create a stronger foundation for the brand as it grows.

The project will be successful when the finished website feels simple, memorable, and easy to navigate. Visitors should immediately recognize the heart hands symbol, understand the brand, and be able to confidently explore and purchase products on any device.

---
## 2. Business Goals

The primary goal of this project is to establish a professional, trustworthy online presence for Hands Heart. The website should communicate that the brand is intentional, established, and committed to the heart hands drawing as its central identity. While the symbol is the focus of the visual experience, the website should communicate this through confident design rather than explicit claims.

The website should also support the sale of products by making them easy to discover, understand, and purchase. The experience should feel simple, approachable, and consistent across desktop and mobile devices, allowing the products and the symbol to remain the primary focus.

More broadly, the website should provide a strong foundation for the future of the brand. As Hands Heart grows through new products, content, and initiatives, the website should be flexible enough to support that growth while maintaining a clear and recognizable identity.

---
## 3. Audience

The primary audience consists of people discovering Hands Heart for the first time through social media, word of mouth, or the products themselves. They may know little about the brand, so the website should quickly communicate a sense of quality, authenticity, and intention.

The website should also serve existing supporters who are already familiar with the heart hands drawing and are looking to browse products, learn more about the brand, or make a purchase. The experience should feel welcoming and uncomplicated, allowing visitors to move naturally from curiosity to confidence.

Regardless of how someone arrives at the website, they should leave with a clear understanding that Hands Heart is a thoughtfully presented lifestyle brand centered around a simple, recognizable symbol. The experience should feel approachable, memorable, and easy to navigate for visitors of all backgrounds.

The website is intentionally simple. Rather than guiding visitors through a large number of pages, the experience will focus on presenting the heart hands symbol, introducing the brand, and making products easy to browse and purchase.

- Home

## 7. Functional Requirements

**Navigation**
- Navigation should remain simple and consistent throughout the website.
- Visitors should be able to access all primary pages within one or two clicks.
- The current page should be clearly indicated.

**Responsive Experience**
- The website must provide a consistent experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- Layouts, typography, and imagery should adapt appropriately to different screen sizes.
- Touch interactions should be optimized for mobile users.

**Shop**
- Visitors should be able to browse the product catalog.
- Visitors should be able to view individual product pages.
- Visitors should be able to add products to their cart.
- Visitors should be able to complete purchases through Shopify.

**Contact**
- Visitors should be able to contact Hands Heart through a simple contact form.
- Form submissions should provide clear confirmation when successfully sent.

**Performance**
- Pages should load quickly on modern devices and internet connections.
- Images should be optimized without noticeably reducing visual quality.
- Navigation and interactions should feel responsive.

**Accessibility**
- Text should be readable and maintain sufficient contrast.
- Interactive elements should be usable with keyboard navigation where applicable.
- Images should include descriptive alternative text when appropriate.

**Search Engine Optimization**
- Every page should include appropriate page titles and meta descriptions.
- Content should be structured to support search engine indexing.
- Social sharing previews should display correctly when links are shared.

**Content Management**
- Products, pricing, and inventory should be managed through Shopify.
- Website content should be organized so future updates can be made without requiring a complete redesign.

## 8. Visual Direction

**Keywords**
- Simple
- Clean
- Confident
- Timeless
- Approachable
- Thoughtful

**Overall Direction**
The website should feel restrained and intentional, allowing the heart hands drawing to become the defining visual element. Every design decision should support the symbol rather than compete with it. Generous whitespace, clear typography, and a minimal layout should create an experience that feels calm, modern, and easy to navigate.

**Typography**
Typography should be clean, contemporary, and highly legible. It should establish a clear hierarchy without drawing attention away from the artwork or products.

**Color**
The visual palette should be predominantly white with black typography and subtle moments of color. Color should be used intentionally to support the brand and products rather than dominate the experience.

**Imagery**
The heart hands drawing should serve as the primary visual asset throughout the website. Product photography should be simple, well-lit, and consistent, allowing the products and the symbol to remain the focus.

**References**
The overall aesthetic should feel closer to a modern gallery or lifestyle brand than a traditional online store. The experience should emphasize clarity, quality, and restraint while allowing the heart hands drawing to become a recognizable and memorable symbol.
