# Service Agreement

This agreement is between LMNL LLC ("Contractor") and Hands Heart LLC ("Client"). This contract is effective upon signature on July 11, 2026.

---
## 1. Project

The Contractor agrees to design and develop the project described in the accompanying Project Requirements Document (PRD). The PRD defines the agreed scope of work, objectives, deliverables, and functional requirements, and is considered part of this agreement.

ref: [[Hands Heart PRD]]

---
## 2. Scope of Work

The Contractor will perform the services outlined in **section 4.a** of the PRD, which includes:

- Website design
- Frontend website development
- Shopify integration
- Responsive optimization for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Basic search engine optimization (SEO)
- Contact form
- Hosting setup
- Domain and DNS configuration
- Website deployment and launch support

The work that is outside the scope, as defined in **section 4.b** of the PRD, includes:
- Brand identity redesign
- Logo or illustration creation
- Product photography
- Copywriting beyond minor refinements
- Ongoing website maintenance
- Marketing campaigns or social media management
- Custom Shopify app development
- Additional features not outlined in the PRD

---
## 3. Deliverables

Upon completion of the project, the Client will receive all deliverables described in the PRD, which includes:

- Completed website
- Source code
- Design assets
- Documentation
- Credentials for applicable services
- Deployment to the agreed hosting environment

---
## 4. Project Timeline

The project will progress through the following phases:

1. Discovery
2. Design
3. Development
4. Review
5. Launch

Estimated timelines are dependent upon timely feedback and delivery of required materials by the Client. Delays in approvals or content will extend the project schedule.

---
## 5. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

- Provide requested content and assets.
- Review submitted work in a timely manner.
- Consolidate revision requests where possible.
- Maintain communication throughout the project.
- Pay invoices according to the agreed payment schedule.

Project delays caused by missing information or delayed feedback will extend the timeline.

---
## 6. Payment

#### Project Fee: $4,250 USD
- $3,500 for the work
- $750 rush fee
#### Deposit: $2,125 USD
#### Remaining Balance: $2,125 USD
#### Payment Schedule
- 50% deposit due before work begins.
- Remaining balance due prior to final deployment and transfer of deliverables.

Invoices are payable within 7 calendar days unless otherwise agreed. Late payments will pause work until the account is current.

---
## 7. Revisions

- The project includes **two rounds** of reasonable revisions during the design and review process.
- Revision requests should be consolidated whenever possible.
- Requests that substantially alter previously approved work or introduce new functionality may be treated as additional work and billed separately.

---
## 8. Changes in Scope

- The Client may request additional work at any time.
- If the requested work falls outside the agreed scope, the Contractor will provide an updated estimate and an amended contract before proceeding.
- No additional work will begin without written approval from the Client.

---
## 9. Ownership

Upon receipt of full payment, ownership of the final project deliverables transfers to the Client.

The Contractor retains ownership of:
- Internal workflows
- Design processes
- Reusable code
- Templates
- Development tools
- General knowledge developed during the project

The Contractor reserves the right to display completed work in portfolios, case studies, and promotional materials unless otherwise agreed in writing.

---
## 10. Warranties

- The Warranty will exist for 14 days after completion and submission of deliverables 
- The Contractor warrants that the delivered work will substantially conform to the agreed requirements at the time of delivery.
- The Contractor does not guarantee uninterrupted operation of third-party services, hosting providers, payment processors, browsers, or future software updates outside their control.

---
## 11. Termination

- Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice.
- The Client remains responsible for payment for all completed work performed prior to termination. Cost of completed work will be itemized and invoiced at fair market value.
- Deposits are non-refundable once work has begun.

---
## 12. Limitation of liability

- The Contractor shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or special damages arising from the use of the delivered work.
- Total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid under this contract.

---
## 13. Acceptance

- The Client will have seven (7) calendar days after delivery to review the completed project.
- If no material issues are reported during this period, the project will be considered accepted.
- Minor corrections identified during the acceptance period will be addressed at no additional charge if they fall within the agreed scope.

---
## 14. Support After Launch

- The project includes a fourteen (14) day post-launch support period for correcting issues directly related to the delivered work.
- Requests for new features, additional pages, content updates, or functionality beyond the agreed scope are considered new work and will be quoted separately.
